#Yay
1. **Install a package**
> yay -S package
1. **Search a package**
> yay -Ss package
1. **Remove a package**
> yay -Rns package
1. **Package selection**
> yay <Search term>
1. **Upgrade a package installed with yay**
> yay -Syu
1. **Clean dependencies**
> yay -Yc
